Welcome to UniteSBY, the place to find youth-focused events, activities, and programs happening in and around the City of Salisbury. This service is free, easy to use, and provides you with date, time, and location of the events. Sign up to post events or check out the calendar or map to view upcoming events nearest you.

UniteSBY is a community-driven effort to strengthen youth culture in our area. Help us spread the word and build this app to its fullest potential!
